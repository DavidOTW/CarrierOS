Required sharing disclosure

Drivers should place this disclosure next to every referral link or recommendation:

Disclosure: I earn a recurring commission when someone subscribes to CarrierOS through my link.

The disclosure must be clear and easy to see. “Affiliate link” by itself may not explain that the driver is paid.
