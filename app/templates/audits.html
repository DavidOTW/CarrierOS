How the comparison works

RC
RateCon

Checks a clearly labeled total rate against load revenue and reports missing driver-dispatch details. It never changes revenue automatically.

BK
Bank statement

Compares statement deposits and withdrawals with modeled load revenue and operating expense for the selected delivery-date period. Timing and transfers still require reconciliation.

BL
Bill statement

Checks a clearly labeled amount due against a recurring overhead estimate and suggests review when the difference is material.

Best bank format: CSV with Date and Amount columns, or separate Debit and Credit columns. PDF extraction only recognizes clearly labeled totals and cannot perform OCR.
